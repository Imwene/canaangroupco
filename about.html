About Us

We strive to raise the bar in every sector we operate in by reimagining what’s possible.

Canaan East Company has a diverse portfolio in various sectors. Every day we endeavor to make our organization an enjoyable place to work, where our employees can collaborate, innovate, grow and realize their potential. We have successfully positioned ourselves within the market by establishing various subsidiaries engaging in different business spheres such as Tourism & Hospitality, Manufacturing, Agro and Food Processing, Construction & Real Estate, as well as Interior Design. We are continuously looking for new ways of adding value to the regions we serve and believe that the future holds immense opportunities for us to make a lasting impact.

Vision

"To be a leader in establishing sustainable enterprises that transform lives."

Mission

To be a world-class enterprise that is passionate about the standards of living in key growth markets in Africa.

Code of conduct

Earning, establishing and retaining trust and confidence inside and outside our business is vital to all activities within and related to the organisation.
