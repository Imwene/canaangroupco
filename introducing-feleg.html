Introducing Feleg: Oil and gas company

by Admin
April, 14

Feleg Africa is a multi-energy company that was incorporated in April 2021. It is engaged in the midstream and downstream trading of refined petroleum products. We have a prodigious focus on sub-Saharan Africa through trading and partnering with local stakeholders to meet all their energy needs. We strategize to source products directly from major refineries and international petroleum and petrochemical companies. Effectively ensuring our products reach our end users safely. Our product portfolio covers a wide range of white fuel products including:

  • Diesel
  • Kerosene
  • Petrol
  • Jet Fuel

    • We are continuously expanding and enhancing our business asset base. Our goal and aim is to solidify our position as one of Kenya’s top distributor of petroleum products.
