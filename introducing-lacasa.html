Introducing Lacasa Complex: A home of elegance and style.

by Admin
April, 14

Furniture is one of the essential requirements of every space and it not only gives comfort but also enhances the look. People want to have a surrounding that induces the feeling of calm, comfort and wellbeing. To live up to its core values, CanaanEast Company continues to set new standards and expand our portfolio with the foundation of Lacasa Complex, A home of elegance and style. It is focused to provide world class furniture to meet this need.
It is set to launch in 2022, with a grand event to make an introduction to the market. These additions take design and flexibility to the next level, amplify style in homes and exceptional comfort. The products by the retailer can be best expressed in the store’s slogan, “Tells the designer and craftsperson’s story”.
